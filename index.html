Built With Passion

MortApps Studios was founded in 2024 by Mort Ian K., a 21-year-old developer with a passion for solving real problems through code.

What started as a one-person freelance journey quickly grew into a studio that has partnered with dozens of businesses across Kenya. We remain a small, dedicated team because we believe in personal connections and craftsmanship.

Every project we take on is approached with the same commitment: understand the problem deeply, build the solution thoughtfully, and deliver something that genuinely helps.
